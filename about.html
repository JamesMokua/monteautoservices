ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Monte Auto Services was founded 13+ years ago with the aim of providing the highest standards of motor vehicle maintenance, repair and restoration services. We deal with speciality brands which are Mercedes Benz, Volkswagen and Nissan


Our workshop is situated along Shimo La Tewa Road, off Lusaka Road, plot 13 and is fully equipped with ample parking, an electric fence all around, security lights & security guards are present 24hrs a day.

PROCESS

The Workshop has a standard procedure for vehicle admittance into the workshop area.


Normal Service Vehicle inspection

  1. Opening of job cards
  2. Client signs agreement of terms & gives authority for repairs
  3. Carrying out of repairs
  4. Payment on vehicle delivery


Accident repairs Vehicle assessment

  1. Estimate quotation
  2. Insurance assessor carries out re-assessment
  3. Issuing of L.P.O
  4. Carrying out of repairs
  5. Signing off on satisfactory work completion
  6. Invoice submission.
