CAR PAINTING & RESTORATION

Painting

Our expert team of paint technicians can take the plastic body parts of your vehicle and paint them to whatever colour you choose. This can be one of the less costly ways to enhance your car, even something as simple as colour coding can make quite a big impact on the overall look of your vehicle.
We can colour code the following parts:

  1. Wing mirrors
  2. Bumpers
  3. Side skirts
  4. Front grilles
  5. Air intakes
  6. Interior trims and mouldings

Vehicle Respraying

We have the facilities to create custom paint colours and carry out a complete vehicle respray. We have many types of paint finishes which enables us to create something truly unique for you.

At Monte Auto Services there is a great range of colours to choose from, we can even mix custom colours if you are into pearlescent and metallic finishes.

Repainting your vehicle is a great way to bring it back up to date if it is looking a little tired or if the paint has many scratches and small dents.
