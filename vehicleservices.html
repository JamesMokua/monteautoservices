The importance of servicing your vehicle regularly

Vehicle components and parts naturally wear out over time, and road hazards can negatively impact critical components. By adhering to preventive maintenance schedules, you can ensure that your vehicle is always runs at optimal conditions.
If frequent servicing is not performed regularly, vehicle life span will be greatly reduced. It helps to avoid or reduce vehicle breakdowns and is based on time, mileage, engine hours, or amount of fuel used. It includes scheduled lubrication, adjustment, cleaning, testing, repair, and/or worn parts replacement.
